Origin Story

Born to a blind king, Duhsasana was raised in the shadow of ambition. His devotion to his brother became his identity. Loyalty turned into obsession.

Psychological Analysis

Duhsasana represents blind loyalty. When obedience overrides morality, ego disguises itself as strength.
